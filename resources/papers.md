## 📖 How to Read AI Papers

1. **Abstract** — Get the gist (30 seconds)
2. **Introduction** — What problem? Why important? (5 minutes)
3. **Figures & Tables** — Visual understanding (5 minutes)
4. **Conclusion** — What did they find? (2 minutes)
5. **Full read** — Only if it's relevant to your work (30-60 minutes)

> 💡 Use tools like [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) and [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) to find related work.
